What is Amazon DSP?

Amazon DSP lets brands programmatically reach audiences across Amazon and third-party sites using Amazon’s powerful shopper data.

Target the Right People, at the Right Time

With Amazon’s powerful shopper data, you can reach people based on what they browse, buy, and search—making your ads more relevant and effective.

Show Up Where It Matters

Run your ads not just on Amazon, but also across trusted sites like IMDb, Fire TV, MX Player and other high-quality apps and websites.

Reach People on Any Device

Whether someone’s on their phone, laptop, or smart TV, Amazon DSP helps you deliver a seamless ad experience across all screens.

Bring Back Interested Shoppers

Reconnect with people who viewed your products or website and find new customers who behave like your best ones.

See What’s Working (and What’s Not)

Get detailed reports that go beyond clicks. See how your ads impact actual shopping behavior and sales on Amazon.

Create Ads That Stand Out

From eye-catching banners to engaging videos and audio ads, you have the freedom to tell your brand story in the format that fits best.
